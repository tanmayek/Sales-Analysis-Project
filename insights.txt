1. Regional Sales Insight:
West region generates the highest revenue, indicating strong market demand.
South and Central regions underperform, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing and expansion strategies.

2. Category Profit Insight:
Technology category generates the highest profit, indicating strong margins.
Furniture has the lowest profitability, suggesting possible over-discounting or higher costs.
This highlights an opportunity to optimize pricing or cost structure in the Furniture category.

3. Discount vs Profit Insight:
Furniture category has the highest average discount and lowest profit, indicating that heavy discounting is reducing profitability.
Technology products maintain lower discounts and higher profit margins, showing stronger pricing power.
Reducing discounts in Furniture could significantly improve profitability.

4. Sales Trend Insight:
Sales fluctuate over time with noticeable spikes during certain months, especially towards the end of the year.
This suggests seasonal demand patterns, likely driven by holidays or promotions.
Businesses should align inventory planning and marketing campaigns with peak periods to maximize revenue.

5. Peak Sales Months Insight:
Top-performing months are concentrated around September, November, and December.
This indicates strong seasonal demand, likely driven by pre-holiday and holiday shopping periods.
Businesses can maximize revenue by increasing inventory, marketing campaigns, and promotions during these peak months.

6. Customer Segment Insight:
Consumer segment generates the highest revenue, indicating strong demand from individual customers.
Corporate and Home Office segments contribute less, suggesting potential growth opportunities through targeted marketing and business-focused offerings.
